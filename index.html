The Divine Mother and Child Health Programme was launched on 1st September in 10 countries in Asia Pacific region. Current Mother care in our rural areas remains to be improved which in turn prevents Pregnancy/Delivery related deaths of both mothers and infants. This much needed medical services will be extended to Rural communities covering all of Chikkaballapur District (6 taluks) taking Love and Care to the door steps.
